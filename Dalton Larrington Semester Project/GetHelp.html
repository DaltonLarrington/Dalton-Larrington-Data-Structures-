Get Help! Don't Wait!

If you are struggling with depression, suicidal thoughts, or just need someone to talk to, get help. Don't wait to get help, there are people out there that are willing to help you through your tough times.


Mental Healthline: If you have Depression

1-855-792-6130


National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: If you have suicidal thoughts

1-800-273-8255


Contact Helpline: If you just need to talk

1-800-932-4616


If you or a loved one, or someone you know is struggling, please refer them to this website. Get them help, don't wait. This websites purpose is help others in their time of need.
